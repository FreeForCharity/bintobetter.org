Free For Charity has a simple mission with broad implications

Reduce costs and increase revenues for nonprofits; putting that money back into their charitable mission where it belongs.

This charity for charities seeks to replace as many functions as possible that current nonprofits pay for to for-profit companies with free or at cost work from our campus, on site projects, or partnerships with other entities.

Frequently Asked Questions

Free For Charity is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization (EIN: 46-2471893) dedicated to supporting other nonprofits by providing free or at-cost services including web hosting, domain names, consulting, and technology assistance.

Any registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization or organization in the process of obtaining 501(c)(3) status is eligible to apply for our services.

Visit our volunteer opportunities page on Idealist.org or contact us directly. We're looking for web developers, designers, marketers, and consultants to help nonprofits succeed.

Contact us through our website or email us directly at info@freeforcharity.org with information about your organization and the services you need.
