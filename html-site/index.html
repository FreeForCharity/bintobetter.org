Our Mission: Reducing Waste, Empowering Youth

BinToBetter transforms hard-to-recycle materials into valuable community resources, creating positive environmental impact while serving schools, seniors, and students.

As a youth-led nonprofit, we turn used tennis balls, e-waste, and hard-to-recycle plastics into practical tools that benefit our community. Through hands-on recycling initiatives and educational workshops, we demonstrate that waste reduction isn't just possible‚Äîit's powerful.

Frequently Asked Questions

BinToBetter is a youth-led nonprofit organization dedicated to reducing community waste by transforming used tennis balls, e-waste, and hard-to-recycle plastics into valuable tools for schools, seniors, and students.

We collect used tennis balls and cut them to create protective covers for walker legs and chair legs. These covers reduce noise in classrooms and assisted living facilities while protecting floors from damage.

Contact us through our website to arrange drop-off or pickup. We accept used tennis balls, electronic devices, and hard-to-recycle plastics. Check our events page for collection drives.

Join us for volunteer opportunities including tennis ball collection, e-waste workshops, and community outreach events. We welcome students passionate about environmental sustainability and hands-on learning.
